About Me

I started this blog to mess around with data science projects and document my progress. If you have any suggestions for projects that would seem to fit here, please let me know!

I have a wide amount of interests, and all of them fall squarely within the geekdom. When I am not working on problem sets or coding, I am usually playing DOTA, reading comics, watching horror films, or writing poetry.
